Why Warmup & Cooldown?

Proper warmups increase blood flow, improve mobility, and reduce injury risk. Cooldowns aid recovery and flexibility.

Tips

  • Always warmup before intense training
  • Never skip cooldown after workouts
  • Stay hydrated throughout
