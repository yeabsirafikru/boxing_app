Weekly Training Schedule

Day 1 - Boxing + Skill

Morning: Boxing technique work (30-45 min)

Evening: Handstand skill practice (15-20 min)

Focus: Clean technique, balance development

Day 2 - Strength + Skill

Morning: Full body strength training (45-60 min)

Evening: Handstand progression work (15-20 min)

Focus: Strength building, skill transfer

Day 3 - Boxing + Skill

Morning: Boxing combination drills (30-45 min)

Evening: Handstand balance work (15-20 min)

Focus: Combination flow, balance consistency

Day 4 - Strength + Skill

Morning: Upper body focus strength (45-60 min)

Evening: Handstand endurance work (15-20 min)

Focus: Upper body development, hold time

Day 5 - Boxing + Skill

Morning: Boxing sparring simulation (40-50 min)

Evening: Handstand freestyle practice (15-20 min)

Focus: Fight simulation, freestanding attempts

Day 6 - Light Skill + Mobility

Morning: Light handstand practice (20-30 min)

Evening: Full body mobility & stretching (30 min)

Focus: Active recovery, flexibility

Day 7 - FULL REST

All Day: Complete rest & recovery

Optional: Light walking, meditation

Focus: Physical & mental recovery

This 7-day structure repeats for the full 30 days. Listen to your body and adjust intensity as needed. Recovery is as important as training.
